科技英语是雅思写作和口语的核心语料库，掌握这组词能让科技类话题作文词汇更丰富、更地道。

**五个核心高频词**：

- **disruptive** /dɪsˈrʌptɪv/：颠覆性的
  *AI is a disruptive force transforming entire industries.*

- **proliferate** /prəˈlɪfəreɪt/：快速扩散、激增
  *Mobile applications proliferate faster than ever before.*

- **alleviate** /əˈliːvieɪt/：减轻、缓解
  *Automation alleviates repetitive and time-consuming workloads.*

- **unprecedented** /ʌnˈpresɪdentɪd/：前所未有的
  *This technology brings unprecedented efficiency to daily life.*

- **infrastructure** /ˈɪnfrəstrʌktʃər/：基础架构
  *Robust infrastructure underpins long-term app stability.*

**综合运用（一句话包含5个词）**：

> The proliferation of AI-driven tools has brought unprecedented disruption to traditional industries, alleviating labor shortages while demanding ever more resilient infrastructure.

这种"一句话堆高频词"的写法是雅思 Band 7+ 的标志性特征。把这五个词背到肌肉记忆级别，写作时信手拈来，评分自然上去。
