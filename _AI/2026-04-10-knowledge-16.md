雅思写作 Task 1 或托福综合写作里，如果只会用 increase / decrease，词汇分会受限。这组精准同义替换，能让表达层次感立刻提升。

**表示激增**
- **surge**（急剧上升，强调速度与幅度）
  > *Mobile payment adoption surged by 60% within two years.*
- **proliferate**（迅速扩散、大量涌现）
  > *AI-powered applications have proliferated across all industries.*

**表示下降**
- **plummet**（暴跌，幅度大且速度快）
  > *The cost of renewable energy has plummeted over the past decade.*
- **dwindle**（逐渐减少至微量，过程缓慢）
  > *Traditional bookstores are dwindling in number nationwide.*

**表示平稳**
- **plateau**（达到峰值后趋于平稳，不再增长）
  > *User growth has plateaued after the initial launch phase.*

**综合练习（一句话串联三种趋势）**

> While smartphone adoption *surged* in emerging markets, desktop sales *plummeted*, and tablet demand *plateaued* in developed economies.

记忆技巧：把词汇嵌入真实语境句子里反复朗读，比背单词表高效 3 倍。下次写作时，有意识地替换掉 increase / decrease，考官一定会注意到！
