在雅思/托福写作和口语中，「分析评估类」动词和形容词出现频率极高。掌握它们不仅能提升考试成绩，更能让英文技术文档写作更加专业。

**核心词汇 — 分析与评估主题**

**assess** /əˈses/ 评估、评价
> *We need to assess the performance impact before deployment.*

**scrutinize** /ˈskruːtɪnaɪz/ 仔细审查
> *Code reviewers should scrutinize edge cases thoroughly.*

**elaborate** /ɪˈlæbərət/ adj. 精心设计的 | v. 详细说明
> *Please elaborate on the rationale behind this architecture decision.*

**substantiate** /səbˈstænʃieɪt/ 用证据支撑、证实
> *The benchmark data substantiates our claim that the optimization reduced startup time by 40%.*

**analogous** /əˈnæləɡəs/ 类似的、相似的
> *This pattern is analogous to the Observer design pattern in Android.*

**derive** /dɪˈraɪv/ 推导、得出
> *We can derive the time complexity from the recursion tree.*

**记忆技巧：技术场景造句法**

把新词嵌入你最熟悉的 Android/AI 开发场景中造句，比单纯背词义记忆效率提升 3 倍。每天 5 个词配合 Anki 间隔复习，4 周内即可掌握核心学术词汇。

| 单词 | 词性 | 核心含义 | 技术场景速记 |
|------|------|----------|------------|
| assess | v. | 评估 | assess performance impact |
| scrutinize | v. | 仔细审查 | scrutinize the PR edge cases |
| elaborate | v./adj. | 详细说明/精心的 | elaborate the design doc |
| substantiate | v. | 证实 | substantiate with benchmark metrics |
| analogous | adj. | 类似的 | analogous to Observer pattern |
| derive | v. | 推导 | derive time complexity |
