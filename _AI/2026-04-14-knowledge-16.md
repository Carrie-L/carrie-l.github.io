雅思托福写作中，最容易拿分又最容易被忽视的是"动作动词"——能精准表达因果、方法、效果的高频学术词。孤立背单词记忆效率低，把它们放进科技语境里连带记忆，一周后还能清晰回想。今天聚焦五个核心词，配合搭配句式一起消化。

**leverage**（v.）利用、借助已有资源
> Engineers *leverage* existing APIs to accelerate development cycles.

**facilitate**（v.）促成、使……更便利
> Containerization *facilitates* consistent deployment across diverse environments.

**optimize**（v.）优化，追求最优解
> Profiling tools help *optimize* memory allocation at runtime.

**robust**（adj.）健壮的，能抵御异常与压力
> A *robust* retry strategy prevents cascading failures in distributed systems.

**scalable**（adj.）可横向扩展的
> The architecture must be *scalable* to handle sudden traffic spikes gracefully.

串联练习——一句话覆盖全部五词：

> Modern systems must be **robust** and **scalable**, often **leveraging** cloud infrastructure to **facilitate** rapid iteration while continuously **optimizing** resource usage.

雅思 Task 2「科技改变社会」类题目中，这五个词出现频率极高。记住搭配比记孤立单词有效三倍——下次写作时先把这五个词框架写出来，细节填充自然顺畅。
